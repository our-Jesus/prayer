Prayer for Divine Scripture Guidance

✠ In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit. Amen.

Come, O Holy Spirit, Divine Guide and Light of Truth. You who inspired the sacred authors to write Your Word, guide me now to the exact passage of Scripture that my soul needs at this moment.

Spirit of Wisdom, direct this selection according to Your perfect will. Lead me not by chance, but by Your divine providence to the verse, chapter, or book that speaks to my heart's deepest need. You know what I require for my spiritual growth before I myself understand it.

O God, as You once guided the apostles to choose Matthias by casting lots, trusting in Your sovereign will, so too do I trust that You will guide this sacred selection. May the Scripture You reveal be a lamp unto my feet and a light unto my path.

Grant that I may receive whatever passage You choose with faith, humility, and an open heart, knowing that Your Word never returns empty but accomplishes Your purpose. Through Christ our Lord. Amen.

"The lot is cast into the lap, but its every decision is from the Lord." - Proverbs 16:33

About This Companion

This Sacred Scripture Companion is available in both English and Malayalam (മലയാളം), allowing Catholics from different linguistic backgrounds to pray and read in their preferred language. The names of the Biblical books are based on the official POC Catholic Bible translation. The tool offers nine distinct spiritual journeys, from the complete Catholic Bible to focused selections like the Gospels, Psalms, Wisdom Literature, and more. When a chapter is selected, both English and Malayalam book names are displayed to serve bilingual communities.

Choose from various sacred collections: the complete 73-book Catholic Bible, the Old Testament's salvation history, the New Testament's Gospel message, the four Gospels about Christ's life, the 150 Psalms for prayer and praise, Wisdom Literature for practical guidance, St. Paul's theological letters, the Prophets' divine messages, or the Pentateuch's foundational books. Each selection offers a unique spiritual focus guided by the Holy Spirit.

The prayer featured is the traditional Catholic invocation to the Holy Spirit that has been used for centuries before reading Sacred Scripture, available in both languages using authentic Catholic prayer formulations. Each selection is made through what we trust to be Divine Providence, allowing the Holy Spirit to guide you to the Scripture passage most needed for your spiritual journey at this moment.
